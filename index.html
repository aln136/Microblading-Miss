Microblading


Microblading is the use of realistic-looking hairs to create natural-looking enhancements. The technique uses a hand-held tool with needles to apply pigments that look like additional hairs. A certified technician will measure your features to determine the right placements. Of course, you can also specify what kind of brow shape you want.

The 3-D eyebrow tattoo technique creates fine, realistic-looking hairs to create a sleek, more filled-in brow shape and volume.

The 4-D eyebrow tattoo technique is a fuller and more detailed approach, which includes more fine volume hairstrokes and the use of a shading technique which creates a more filled-in effect.
