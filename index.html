Services

Microblading


Microblading is the use of realistic-looking hairs to create natural-looking enhancements. The technique uses a hand-held tool with needles to apply pigments that look like additional hairs. Your features will be measured to determine the right placements. Of course, you can also specify what kind of brow shape you want.

The 3-D eyebrow tattoo technique creates fine, realistic-looking hairs to create a sleek, more filled-in brow shape and volume.

The 4-D eyebrow tattoo technique is a fuller and more detailed approach, which includes more fine volume hairstrokes and the use of a shading technique which creates a more filled-in effect.

Eyeliner


Permanent eyeliner defines your eyes and makes your eyelashes appear darker and fuller. It eliminates the need to apply eyeliner manually every morning completely. This procedure enhances eye symmetry and eye color, and gives the look of larger and brighter eyes. This treatment is 100% waterproof and smudge-proof.

For clients who want a more natural look, the eyeliner will just enhance the base of the lashes to give a full and thick appearance. For those who want a bolder look, eyeliner can be built up to the customer’s desired thickness over the course of multiple sessions.
