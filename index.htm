Is Microblading For You?

Microblading is a form of semi-permanent makeup for people who are unsatisfied with the shape or density of their eyebrows. The procedure involves the use of needles to implant pigment under the skin to create a look of naturally full and soft brows. The strokes are applied finely to achieve a defined and multidimensional result.

Microblading


Microblading is the use of realistic-looking hairs to create natural-looking enhancements. The technique uses a hand-held tool with needles to apply pigments that look like additional hairs. A certified technician will measure your features to determine the right placements.

Eyebrows


Semi-permanent makeup is the application of pigment to the eyebrows, eyes, lips, or other areas of the face . The purpose of semi-permanent makeup is to give a soft, natural look and to correct scars or other asymmetrical features of the face.

The 3-D eyebrow tattoo technique creates fine, realistic-looking hairs to create a sleek, more filled-in brow shape and volume.

The 4-D eyebrow tattoo technique is a fuller and more detailed approach, which includes more fine volume hairstrokes and the use of a shading technique which creates a more filled-in effect.
